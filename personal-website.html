WHO AM I

My name is Zirc Albon


I Create, Innovate, and Develop!


I took up BS in ComSci for two years, and I dropped out.
There are a lot of reasons why students in the Philippines can't finish their studies due to financial and other personal issues, I'm one of them and decided to work instead.

I keep honing my skills through self-study, tutorial videos, online courses, and boot camps while working at the same time.

To sum up, I worked as a Reservation Officer & as an IT Staff at first then became a Graphic Designer, to an IT consultant, a Database administrator, a Developer, and a Service Desk Supervisor.

My journey is not easy, but I'm glad that my career is all IT-related, I know it's hard, but I did it eventually!

I never stop upskilling
and as a matter of fact,
I have an ongoing / currently enrolled in a Development Bootcamp.
